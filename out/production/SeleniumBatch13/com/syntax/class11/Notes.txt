ACTIONS CLASS:
     Is given by selenium to capture keyboard and mouse events such as:
     move to element
     drag and drop
     double click
     right click
Click is to actually click on the element
Perform is to perform a certain action from the actions class

If regular click does not work you can use the alternative way
of having Actions class click method

UPLOAD DOCUMENTS:
